03 — Approach

Transform the scattered client requests into a bi-weekly, demo-focused expert series featuring speakers and content provided by MSFT/GitHub, while customers lead internal engagement efforts, supported by a customer engineer demonstration to enhance peer-to-peer learning.

Cadence

  • Bi-weekly meetings featuring a specific topic and demonstrations presented by Microsoft and GitHub speakers.
  • The strategic customer manages the internal aspects such as audience coordination, promotion, and community engagement, while Microsoft and GitHub provide the speakers and content. For non-strategic customers, sessions can be conducted in a shared webinar format.
  • Speakers from Microsoft and GitHub rotate regularly, including GitHub Sales Engineers/CSAs and Microsoft SEs, CSAs, GBBs, and Developer Advocates.

Format

  • A one-hour meeting led by the customer sponsor or champion, requiring at least 200 attendees.
  • Customer champions participate to facilitate the discussion and provide relevant customer insights.
  • We highlight our skills development opportunities, including the ESI program and Microsoft Learn.
